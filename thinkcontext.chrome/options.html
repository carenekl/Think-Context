Welcome to thinkContext, the web browser extension which adds contextually relevant, politically progressive information to your web browsing. For more information visit our website at thinkcontext.org.

As you browse the web thinkContext consults several progressive data sources and if it finds anything relevant to what you are looking at it will unobtrusively add that information right into the page. Look for the icons shown next to the data sources below and hover your mouse over them to show more information.

Below you may configure which data sources thinkContext consults. We recommend to begin with that you enable all data sources and only disable one if you have a specific reason.


Progressive News Trackback - See what progressive blogs and news sources are saying about the mainstream news you are reading. thinkContext scans over 50 progressive news sources such as The Nation, Earthjustice, Colorlines, and Feministing. For a full list see here.
Union Hotels - Using the Union Hotel Guide put out by Hotel Workers Rising this feature will show you which hotels have union workers and which are involved involved in a labor dispute.
Green Business Information - highlights companies listed in The Green Pages.
